Help

Website Home Page: Check-Up

Press the start check up button to start answering questions relevant to your mental health.

Answer the questions according to the scale by selecting one of the five buttons closest to your response. Pick the left or right buttons if you strongly relate with the word, the middle if you feel neutral, and the middle left or right if you only somewhat relate to the word.

You will then receive a final score based of how the questions were answered, as well as a direct link to a recommended page on the website.

Breathing Exercise Page

Press the start exercise button for the circle animation to start.

Follow the circle, inhaling while it grows, holding your breath for as long as the circle stays, and then exhaling as the circle shrinks. The exercise continues for two minutes, until the circle stops moving.

Please reload the page to reset the breathing exercise.

Screen Timer Page

Press the begin timer button to start the 10 minute timer. It is recommended that you do not interact with any screens for the duration of the timer.

There is a timer bar as well to visually display the countdown timer.

Please reload the page to reset the screen timer.

Hotlines Page

The hotlines page includes two phone numbers to call in case you need to talk to someone. Don’t be afraid to reach out for help.

Donate Page

The donate page includes direct links to organizations where you can donate to and help with mental health issues. Clicking on the links will direct you to the organizations’ donation pages.

Navigation

There is a navigation bar at the top of every page where you can directly go to any page on the website (not including the question pages). Feel free to explore the website and try out the other activities.
